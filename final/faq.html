FAQs

When does your menu change?

A: Our menu changes every 2 weeks. Join our mailing list to be notified!

Why only 5 menu items?

A: We want to make dining out easier and often the hardest thing is deciding what to eat. 5 items give variety but not too much to be overwhelming.

Will you bring back a dish?

A: Absolutely! Popular dishes will be rotated in so stay tuned!

How do I cancel my reservation?

A: Your confirmation email will have a link to cancel your reservation. We understand that life happens, but we ask, if possible, to cancel 24 hours in advance.

Do you cater?

A: Unfortunately, no
